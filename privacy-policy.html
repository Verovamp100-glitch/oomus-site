Privacy Policy

Last updated — June 2026

OoMuS ("we," "our," "us") is an AI creative agency. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or engage our services, we may collect your name, email address, company name, and any project details you provide. We collect this information only when you voluntarily share it — through email, contact forms, or direct communication.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to enquiries, deliver creative services, send project-related communications, and improve our offerings. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics tools to understand visitor behaviour — such as pages viewed and time spent on site. These tools may place cookies on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools for hosting, analytics, or communication (such as email providers). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We only share information with third parties to the extent necessary to deliver our services.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. Project files are retained for a reasonable period after delivery to support revisions and ongoing collaboration.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
